About Campus Reports

Campus Reports is a student-first platform that helps universities respond faster to everyday campus problems.

It gives students a simple way to report issues, request maintenance, and track the progress of each request from submission to resolution. Whether it is a broken facility, an urgent safety concern, or a maintenance problem, Campus Reports makes it easier for students to be heard and for the institution to act.

The platform is built around three core goals:

  • Anonymous reporting so students can speak up safely
  • Fast maintenance requests for facilities and campus services
  • Real-time tracking so every report can be followed to completion
