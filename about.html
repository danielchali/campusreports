About Campus Reports

Empowering students to shape a better campus.

Campus Reports is a student-first platform that helps universities respond faster to everyday campus problems. It gives students a simple way to report issues, request maintenance, and track the progress of each request from submission to resolution.

Anonymous Reporting

Submit reports without revealing your identity, so students can speak up safely about any campus concern.

Fast Maintenance

Request repairs and facility support quickly, helping the university maintain a safe and comfortable environment.

Real-Time Tracking

Follow every issue from submission to resolution with live status updates and transparent progress tracking.

Our Mission

Why Campus Reports Exists

Students at Kapasa Makasa University face challenges every day — broken facilities, safety concerns, and issues that often go unreported. Campus Reports was built to close that gap between students and the people who can help.

The platform is built around three core goals: giving students a voice through anonymous reporting, streamlining maintenance requests so problems get fixed faster, and providing full transparency through real-time tracking so no report falls through the cracks.
